About Farmers Market

Who Are We

Farmers Market is a web based application that allows the general public to buy farm produce directly from the farm.
Mission: Our mission is to bridge the gap between the consumers and the farmers. Due to the continued increase in inflation, the cost of consumables have become unbearable and people are dearly in need of a better means of buying their consumables. Creating a platform that allow people to buy their consumables in bulk directly from the farm will give them the opportunity to buy at a cheaper rate. Hence reducing the effect of inflation on our users and enabling them to save more money.
Vision: Our vision is to be among the leading successful e-commerces in the world that brings comfort and happiness to its users.
